ABOUT LAGOS HUB

The Global Shapers Community, Lagos Hub is one of the multi-communities initiated by the World Economic Forum with membership of young people; identified and selected due to their achievement and exceptional leadership potential to shape their communities through social and professional endeavours. The fast-paced nature of our city, Lagos reflects the dynamism in our activities, vision for the hub and our country at large. Drawn from business, nonprofit, public service across major sectors of the Nigerian economy, the hub also plays host to numerous transiting shapers and investors alike, all attempting to get a share of a promising market. This is why we take responsibility to shape the minds of young people, teenagers and influence ecosystems through our initiatives in order to ensure a brighter and more rewarding future for us all. Our initiatives are hinged on: Entrepreneurship and Innovation, Environmental Sustainability, Education and Future of Works, Diversity and Inclusion, Sustainable Cities among other strategic partnerships with many local and international organisations.
